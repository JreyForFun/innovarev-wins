Fashion Store Redesign

E-Commerce • Web Design

The Challenge

The client, a growing fashion retailer, faced declining sales due to an outdated, slow website that performed poorly on mobile devices. They needed a complete overhaul to improve user experience and conversion rates.

The Solution

We migrated their platform to Shopify and designed a custom theme focused on visual storytelling and speed. We optimized the checkout process, implemented advanced product filtering, and ensured a seamless mobile experience.

  • Custom Shopify Theme
  • Mobile-First Design
  • Speed Optimization (90+ score)
  • SEO Migration Strategy

Key Results

150% Increase in Sales
40% Lower Bounce Rate

Technologies Used

Shopify Liquid JavaScript SASS
